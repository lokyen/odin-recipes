Garlic Bread

Ingredients Used

  • 1 16 ounce loaf Italian bread or French bread
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large cloves garlic, smashed and minced
  • 1 heaping tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Cut loaf in half, horizontally. Mis the butter, garlic, and parsley in small bowl.
  3. Spread butter mixture over the two bread halves
  4. Heat in oven for 10 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle parmesan cheese over bread if desired. Return to oven. Boil on high heat for 2-3 minutes until edges of bread start to toast.
  6. Remove from oven and let it cool for a minute. Remove from pan and use bread knife to cut into 1 inch thick slices.
